About Madamfo Africa

Recyclying · Health · Innovation · Advocacy

Madamfo is a social enterprise start-up that aims to bridge the gap between emergency situations and medical help. Our company uses a holistic approach by integrating climate action into our product design and production process, utilizing recycling to reduce environmental impact and conserve resources. Our product design includes the integration of a blood pressure monitor and temperature sensor, allowing customers to easily check their vital signs by placing a finger on the device and reading the results on the display.

Established in November 2019, Madamfo sells first aid kits and educates people on basic first aid knowledge and advocacy. Our goal is to establish a long-term first aid behavior among our target customers, starting with the introduction of our niche product, the environmentally-produced first aid kit. The current market for first aid services is poorly served and inconsistent, with little emphasis on basic first aid knowledge.

We offer eco-friendly first aid kits that come in four different varieties: portable, baby, regular, and premium. Our diverse product offerings and eco-friendly packaging set us apart from our competitors. As a socially responsible company, we donate 2% of the profits from every first aid kit sold to rural communities with limited access to healthcare. Our prices are moderate, ranging from 60gh to 250gh cedi depending on the kit specifications, and we also offer refill services for our eco-friendly boxes. Each kit also comes with a brochure containing detailed instructions for use.

Madamfo is the go-to brand for first aid needs, and we are dedicated to providing the highest level of customer service, including a call line for assistance and clarification.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to offer a product made from recycled waste that enhances safety at all times by facilitating proper response to emergencies and everyday injuries.
